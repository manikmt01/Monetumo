Premium Formats

High-performing ad units with increased CPM.

Slide-in

A subtle, non-intrusive ad that smoothly appears from the side of the screen as users scroll through your content. Ideal for grabbing attention without disrupting the reading experience. It performs exceptionally well for mid-session engagement and call-to-action campaigns.

Recommended content

A personalized ad format that suggests relevant articles, products, or offers based on user interests and browsing behavior. It appears naturally at the end of articles or within content feeds, keeping visitors engaged longer while increasing page views and ad revenue.

Exit Pop-up

Triggered when a user attempts to leave the page, offering a final chance to convert visitors. Commonly used for promoting special deals, newsletter sign-ups, or re-engagement campaigns to reduce bounce rates and increase retention.
