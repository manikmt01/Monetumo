How Header Bidding Works

Header bidding is an advanced programmatic advertising technique that allows multiple ad exchanges to bid on your ad inventory simultaneously, ensuring you get the highest possible price for each ad impression.

The Process Explained

  • 1 Multiple ad networks (Pubmatic, Criteo, Magnite, Index, Google) compete simultaneously for your ad space
  • 2 Each network submits their best bid in real-time before your page loads
  • 3 The highest bidder wins and their ad is displayed to your visitor
  • 4 You receive maximum revenue for every single ad impression

Why This Matters

Traditional AdSense only uses Google's demand. Header bidding opens your inventory to 15+ premium demand partners, creating true competition and driving up your ad prices by up to 60%.
